Initiated in 2024 by Baptiste Fluzin (external link), Studio.DPI is a european collective that uses design and technology to support journalists and NGOs involved in the production of investigative reporting (including but not limited to OSINT). What we do: design and development of tools supporting investigations (data analysis, automated image processing, collaborative writing and editing), interactive maps and visuals, graphic identity, publication follow-up...
