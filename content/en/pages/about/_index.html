Initiated in 2024 by Baptiste Fluzin, Studio.DPI is a european based design&tech collective, bringing together professionals keen to help journalists and NGOs involved in the production of investigative reporting (OSINT, but not limited to). What we do: design and development of investigative assistance tools (data analysis, automated image processing, collaborative writing and editing), interactive mapping and visuals, graphic identity, publication follow-up...
